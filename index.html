Festa de Fraccions!

En aquesta Situació d'Aprenentatge, treballarem amb les fraccions. En realitat, arribats a aquest punt ja hauríeu de saber algunes coses (la tasca 1 s'hauria de saber fer).

Veureu que la web està separada en distints apartats, anirem fent les diferents tasques per aprendre coses noves cada dia.

In this Learning Situation, we will work with fractions. In fact, by now, you should already know some things (Task 1 should be something you can do).

You will see that the website is divided into different sections, and we will complete the various tasks each day to learn new things.

For each section, you can click the buttons "English, Please!" or "En Català" to view the same website in either or the two languages.
